About Indigo Gutter Cleaning

Local gutter cleaning you can trust in the Flathead Valley

Our Mission

Protect Your Home with Reliable Gutter Cleaning

Indigo Gutter Cleaning is a locally owned and operated gutter cleaning company serving the Flathead Valley, including Kalispell, Whitefish, Columbia Falls, Bigfork, Lakeside, Somers, and surrounding communities.

The company was built around a simple idea: Protect people's homes by doing careful, reliable gutter cleaning the right way.

When gutters clog, water ends up where it shouldn't — damaging roofs, foundations, siding, landscaping, and hardscaping. Regular gutter maintenance protects your property from those problems.

A Fifth-Generation Montanan

Indigo Gutter Cleaning is owned and operated by a 5th-generation Montanan who has lived in the Flathead Valley his entire life.

Growing up in Northwest Montana creates a deep appreciation for hard work, taking care of property, helping neighbors, and protecting homes from the elements.

Living in this region means understanding the heavy fall leaves, pine needles, winter snow, and spring runoff that regularly clog gutters in the Flathead Valley. Local experience matters when it comes to maintaining homes in Montana's climate.

Why Indigo Gutter Cleaning Was Started

Before starting Indigo Gutter Cleaning, the owner worked cleaning windows and gutters with Wapiti Window Cleaning.

During that time it became clear that many homeowners struggled to find a reliable, high-quality gutter cleaning service that fully cleared downspouts, removed debris neatly, communicated clearly, and took care of the property.

Indigo Gutter Cleaning was started to fill that gap.

Our Mission

Provide dependable gutter cleaning that protects homes and gives property owners peace of mind.

Starting the business was also motivated by something personal — supporting a growing family, with a wife, child, and another baby on the way. Serving customers well is what allows the business to support that family.

Experience With High-End Homes

The Flathead Valley is home to some of the most beautiful properties in Montana, including large homes around Flathead Lake and in Whitefish.

Working on these homes requires extra care. Indigo Gutter Cleaning is experienced in navigating intricate landscaping, expensive exterior features, delicate rooflines, and complex gutter systems.

Respecting the home and the property is always a top priority.
