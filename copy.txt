Downspout Unclogging
Clearing Blocked Downspouts So Water Drains Properly
A gutter system only works if the downspouts are completely clear.
During every cleaning we verify that downspouts are draining properly by:
clearing debris from the gutter outlet


dropping a ball through the downspout to confirm it is clear


flushing with water when necessary


For more difficult clogs we may use:
flexible pipe snakes


blowers


partial disassembly if necessary


We can attempt to clear underground downspouts, but our primary equipment is designed for above-ground drainage systems.
If your gutters are overflowing or water pours out of the sides, a clogged downspout is often the cause.

Gutter Cleaning FAQ
Common Questions About Gutter Cleaning in the Flathead Valley
Homeowners often have questions about gutter maintenance, cost, and how often cleaning is needed. Below are answers to the most common questions we hear from customers in Kalispell, Whitefish, Columbia Falls, and the surrounding Flathead Valley.

How Much Does Gutter Cleaning Cost?

How Often Should Gutters Be Cleaned in the Flathead Valley?
In Northwest Montana, gutters should usually be cleaned at least every other year.
Many homes require cleaning once or twice per year depending on:
the types of trees nearby


roof design


length of the gutter system


amount of pine needles and leaves falling on the roof


Homes surrounded by trees often benefit from annual or twice-per-year gutter cleaning.

What Are the Signs My Gutters Need Cleaning?
Some common signs that your gutters may be clogged include:
water pouring over the edges during rain


plants or grass growing in the gutters


sagging or heavy gutters


downspouts that do not drain


visible debris buildup


If you notice any of these signs, it’s usually time to schedule a cleaning.

Can Clogged Gutters Cause Winter Damage?
Yes.
In Montana’s climate, debris in gutters can trap water that later freezes. This frozen debris can:
damage gutters


contribute to ice buildup


cause gutters to pull away from the house


Keeping gutters clean before winter helps prevent ice-related damage and drainage problems.

Do Gutter Guards Eliminate the Need for Gutter Cleaning?
Sometimes — but not always.
Gutter guards can be helpful in certain situations, but they are often sold to homeowners who will not benefit much from them.
In many cases:
debris still collects on top of the guards


small debris gets underneath the guards


downspouts can still become clogged


This sometimes leads to gutter cleaners declining service because guard systems make cleaning more difficult.
When gutter guards are properly matched to a home and tree type, they can reduce or sometimes eliminate the need for frequent cleaning.

Can Homeowners Clean Their Own Gutters?
Some homeowners choose to clean their own gutters, but safety should always come first.
If you decide to do it yourself:
verify that downspouts are fully cleared


use a ladder stabilizer that holds the ladder off the gutter


never use sharp tools that can damage or puncture the gutter


never climb a ladder you don’t feel safe on


Falls from ladders are one of the most common household injuries, so caution is extremely important.

How Long Does Gutter Cleaning Take?
The time required depends on the size of the home and the amount of debris.
A cleaning may take:
30 minutes for small homes


several hours for larger homes


most of a day for large or heavily clogged systems


Homes that have not been cleaned in several years typically take longer because debris must be removed by hand.

Do I Need To Be Home During Gutter Cleaning?
No.
Most homeowners do not need to be home while the gutters are being cleaned.
As long as we have access to the exterior of the home and the gutters, the work can usually be completed without anyone present.

How Do You Protect Landscaping and Property?
Protecting the property is a top priority.
We do this by:
moving carefully around landscaping and outdoor features


placing debris on tarps or bagging it when necessary


planning an exit path for debris so it does not spread across the yard


Working slowly and intentionally helps prevent mess and property damage.

When Should I Schedule Gutter Cleaning?
It’s best to schedule early rather than waiting until the last minute.
For best availability:
Spring cleanings: schedule in January or February


Fall cleanings: schedule in July or August


Scheduling ahead helps ensure your gutters are cleaned before peak debris seasons.

When Is the Best Time to Clean Gutters in the Fall?
In the Flathead Valley, the ideal window is usually October through November.
This timing balances:
waiting for most leaves to fall


cleaning before heavy snow arrives



When Should Gutters Be Cleaned in the Spring?
Spring gutter cleaning should happen after snow has melted off the roof.
This removes debris that accumulated during winter and ensures gutters are ready for spring rain and snowmelt runoff.

What Is the Worst Gutter Problem You See?
The worst situations typically happen when gutters have been neglected for many years.
In those cases, the gutters can fill with:
compacted dirt


plants and grass


heavy debris buildup


This material often has to be broken apart and removed by hand, which takes much longer and may already have caused significant damage to the gutter system.
Regular maintenance helps prevent these situations entirely.
